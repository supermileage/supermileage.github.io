Sponsor Us

Become a sponsor

Why sponsor us? Because it is easy and there are a lot of benefits:

  • We issue tax receipts and business acknowledgements
  • We speak at many school, public, and engineering events
  • We can come to speak at any of your events
  • We promote sustainability and minimizing environmental impact
  • We help develop hard-working, well-rounded students
  • There are possibilities of logo placement on the vehicle, website and promotional materials
  • We have great relationships with our existing sponsors

Learn more

With our proven track record and previous media coverage, sponsoring us is a great way to show that your company is helping to support an environmental Canadian student engineering project.

If you would like to learn more about sponsoring the team, please take a look at our Sponsorship Package or contact us at sponsor@supermileage.ca
