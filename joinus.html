Vehicle Mechanics

In the rear, with the gear

Vehicle Mechanics in charge of both the design and manufacture of key external and internal structural parts such as:

  1. Vehicle Aerodynamic Outer Shell
  2. Composite Chassis
  3. Steering system
  4. Brakes system
  5. Safety features: Harness, Firewall & Roll Bar

Electrical

Tesla's Brainchild

The Electrical Division is responsible for designing the electric components of the vehicles and validation testing of all required electrical systems. The main divisional goal for this year is to complete the conversion of the Urban Concept Vehicle to be a battery electric vehicle and to get the electrical systems of the car into race condition.
